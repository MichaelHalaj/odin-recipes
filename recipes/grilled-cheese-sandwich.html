Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Grilled cheese sandwich made in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a class hot sandwich.

Ingredients

    Cheese Options

  • Sliced Cheddar (sharp Cheddar, mild Cheddar, or both)
  • American cheese
  • Gruyere
  • Brie
  • Fontina
  • Gouda
  • Pepper Jack
  • Havarti

    • Bread Options

  • Plain white sandwich bread
  • Sourdough
  • Brioche
  • Ciabatta

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
